Common Questions

We've Got
Answers.

Can't find your answer? Our team picks up every call — a real human, every time.

📞 Call 800.955.1086
How quickly can you get to my home for AC repair?+
With 65 service trucks across South Florida, we offer same-day service in most cases. Call us at 800.955.1086 and we'll give you an honest ETA. Weekends and holidays are included.
How much does AC repair cost in South Florida?+
Our AC diagnostic starts at $29.95 (includes the trip fee). Minor repairs: $150–$400. Major components (compressor, coil): $600–$2,000. We provide a clear upfront quote before any work begins. No surprises.
Do you offer a warranty on your repairs?+
Yes. Every part we replace comes with a 1-year parts warranty. Select AC installations include up to 5 years of labor protection. If anything goes wrong after our repair, we come back and make it right.
Is Pride AC the same as "Pride Home Warranty"?+
"Pride Home Warranty" is a separate national company based in Delaware — not us. They use subcontractors and charge $75–$125 co-pays per call. We are Pride Air Conditioning & Plumbing — local, family-owned South Florida since 1974, with our own in-house techs and no co-pays.
Are your technicians background-checked and uniformed?+
Absolutely. Every Pride technician is a direct employee — never a subcontractor — fully background-checked before joining our team. They arrive in Pride uniforms and marked vehicles so you always know who's at your door.
What AC brands do you service and install?+
We're authorized dealers for Trane, Rheem, Daikin, and Mitsubishi. We also service Carrier, Lennox, Goodman, York, American Standard, Fujitsu, Gree, Coleman, Amana, and more. If it cools, we service it.
