The Pride Story

Built on a
Family Promise.

Barry Pearl founded Pride Air Conditioning in 1974 with one truck, one employee — himself — and a simple promise to South Florida homeowners: fix it right and charge a fair price. No shortcuts. No upselling. Just honest work.

His son, Howard Pearl, grew up in the business — literally. As a teenager, Howard rode on late-night emergency calls, holding a flashlight for his father while learning the trade from the ground up. He worked every position in the company over 20+ years before taking the helm as CEO.

Today, Pride has 125+ employees, 65 service trucks, and two offices serving five South Florida counties. But the philosophy hasn't changed in half a century: answer the phones fast, fix the problem, and don't overcharge.

Howard's goal remains simple: "We want to be your family's HVAC company for life — just like my father was for so many South Florida families."

Speed & Availability

Same-day service 7 days a week. When your AC fails at 95°F in South Florida, waiting two days is not an option. 65 trucks means we almost always get to you today.

We tell you the price before we pick up a tool. No bait-and-switch, no inflated diagnostic fees, no unnecessary upsells. Just honest work at a fair price.

Every repair is backed by a 1-year parts warranty. Every installation is done to manufacturer specs and properly permitted. We don't cut corners — ever.
