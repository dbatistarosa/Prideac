Our 20-Point
Tune-Up Inspection.

Every Pride tune-up follows the same comprehensive checklist — the same inspection we'd want done on our own homes.

✓
Check & adjust refrigerant levels

Proper refrigerant charge is critical to efficiency and capacity.

✓
Clean evaporator coil

Dirt reduces cooling capacity by up to 30%.

✓
Clean condenser coil

Outdoor coil dirt raises compressor head pressure and causes failure.

✓
Inspect & test capacitors

Capacitors are the #1 repair item in South Florida. We flag weak ones before failure.

✓
Lubricate all moving parts

Reduces friction on motors and bearings, extending lifespan significantly.

✓
Check & clear condensate drain

Florida humidity means drain lines clog constantly. We clear and treat with algaecide.

✓
Inspect electrical connections

Loose or corroded connections cause failures. We tighten and treat all contacts.

✓
Test & calibrate thermostat

Ensures accurate temperature readings and proper system staging.

✓
Inspect air filter & airflow

Restricted airflow is a leading cause of coil freezing and compressor failure.

✓
Measure supply/return air temperature

Verifies proper Delta-T performance — the real measure of system efficiency.
