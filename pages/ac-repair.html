Common AC Problems We Fix

We Fix Every
AC Problem.

Our technicians carry parts for all major brands and arrive ready to diagnose and repair on the first visit. Most repairs completed same day.

❄️

Not Cooling

Low refrigerant, dirty coils, failing compressor, or clogged filter. We diagnose the root cause, not just the symptom. Our $29.95 diagnostic pinpoints it.

💧

Leaking Water

Usually a clogged condensate drain line. Left untreated, this causes mold and water damage. We clear and treat drain lines on the spot.

🔊

Strange Noises

Banging, squealing, or rattling means loose components, failing bearings, or a debris-hit fan blade. We identify and fix the source.

💨

Poor Airflow

Blocked ductwork, failing blower motor, or a clogged filter can drastically reduce airflow. We test and restore proper system pressure.

💡

Won't Turn On

Tripped breaker, failed capacitor, bad contactor, or thermostat issue. We carry all common capacitors and contactors on every truck.

Frequently Asked

AC Repair
FAQ.

How much does AC repair cost in South Florida?+
Our diagnostic starts at $29.95 including the trip fee. Minor repairs typically run $150–$400. Major component replacements (compressor, evaporator coil) range $600–$2,000 depending on brand and system size. We always give a clear upfront quote before starting.
How long does an AC repair take?+
Most common repairs (capacitors, refrigerant recharge, drain clogs, contactors) are completed in 1–2 hours. Our trucks are stocked with parts for all major brands, so we fix most issues on the first visit without a return trip.
Why is my AC running but not cooling?+
The most common causes: low refrigerant (leak), dirty coils, a failing compressor, restricted airflow from a clogged filter, or an oversized system with humidity issues. In South Florida's coastal environment, salt-air corrosion on outdoor coils is also frequent. Our diagnostic pinpoints it.
Should I repair or replace my AC?+
If the repair cost exceeds 50% of a new system's cost AND your unit is 10+ years old, replacement often makes more financial sense. We give you an honest recommendation — we never push unnecessary replacements to make a sale.
