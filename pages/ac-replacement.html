Time to Replace?

Signs You Need
a New System.

📅

10+ Years Old

South Florida's heat accelerates wear. Systems over 10 years old lose efficiency rapidly. Newer units use 30–40% less electricity — the savings often offset replacement costs.

💸

High Electric Bills

If your electric bill has climbed steadily year over year while usage hasn't changed, your AC's efficiency has likely degraded significantly — a common sign of aging equipment.

🔧

Frequent Repairs

If you're calling for AC repair more than once a year, the cumulative repair cost often exceeds a new system's price within just 2–3 years of continued repairs.

🌡️

Uneven Temperatures

Hot and cold spots throughout your home indicate a system that can no longer properly manage South Florida's heat load — often an oversizing or capacity issue.

💨

Humidity Problems

Excessive indoor humidity even when the AC runs constantly usually means your system is oversized or losing dehumidification capacity — a serious mold risk in Florida.

❄️

Uses R-22 Refrigerant

R-22 (Freon) is no longer manufactured and costs $80–$120/lb when available. If your system uses R-22, replacement is typically the most cost-effective solution.

How much does a new AC system cost in South Florida?+
A complete AC replacement (equipment + labor + permits) typically ranges from $4,500 to $12,000 depending on system size (tons), SEER rating, brand, and installation complexity. We provide free in-home quotes with no obligation and no pressure.
How long does a new AC installation take?+
Most residential replacements are completed in 4–6 hours. Our crews handle permit coordination, equipment delivery, removal of your old system, and complete installation — typically in a single day.
What size AC do I need for my South Florida home?+
This depends on square footage, ceiling height, insulation quality, window placement, and sun exposure. We perform a full Manual J heat load calculation before recommending any system. We never guess or default to an oversized unit — oversizing creates humidity problems.
Do you offer financing on new AC systems?+
Yes. We offer 0% interest financing options on qualifying AC system purchases. Ask about current financing promotions when you call for your free quote at 800.955.1086.
