Our IAQ Services

Better Air.
Better Life.

South Florida's climate creates unique indoor air quality challenges — high humidity, mold risk, and year-round AC use. We have solutions for every concern.

☀️

UV Light Systems

Germicidal UV lights installed inside your air handler kill bacteria, viruses, mold spores, and VOCs as air passes through — highly effective in South Florida's humid climate. From $199 installed.

🌀

Duct Cleaning

Professional cleaning of your entire duct system — supply and return ducts, grilles, and evaporator coil. Removes dust, debris, allergens, and mold buildup. From $349 for up to 7 vents.

💧

Humidity Control

Whole-home dehumidifiers keep indoor humidity at the ideal 40–55% range, preventing mold growth, musty odors, and structural damage caused by South Florida's year-round humidity.

🧪

Air Purification

Whole-home HEPA and activated carbon filtration systems remove particles, allergens, pet dander, smoke, and chemical odors from every room — not just where the filter sits.

🌿

CO & Gas Safety

Carbon monoxide detectors, gas leak sensors, and proper ventilation solutions for homes with gas appliances, attached garages, or any combustion source.

📊

Air Quality Testing

Professional assessment to identify specific pollutants, mold spores, VOCs, and allergens present in your home before recommending targeted solutions — no guesswork.
