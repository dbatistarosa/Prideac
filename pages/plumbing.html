Plumbing FAQ

Common
Questions.

How much does a plumber cost in South Florida?+
Service calls start at $85–$150 for diagnosis. Drain cleaning averages $150–$350. Water heater replacements range $800–$2,500 depending on size and type. We always quote before starting any work — no surprises.
Do you offer emergency plumbing service?+
Yes. Call 800.955.1086 for active leaks, burst pipes, or sewage backups. A real person answers and dispatches immediately.
Is plumbing covered under your home warranty?+
Yes. Our home warranty plans include drain cleaning, pipe repairs, fixture replacements, and water heater service — all with no co-pay. See our Home Warranty page for full details.
Do you use subcontractors?+
Never. Every plumber who comes to your home is a direct Pride employee — licensed, background-checked, uniformed, and trained to our standards.
